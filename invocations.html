PROMINENCE INVOCATIONS

Prominence features a Astral Invocations Spellbook with 38 possible spells to cast. Below is a guide on how to cast all of them and what each spell does. Each spell is cast by invoking one of the three invocations known as Gon(Arcane), Heo(Frost), and Rah(Fire). Your Astral Invocation(primary spell) is used to actually cast the manually configured spell.

Pearl Summon: Gon + Astral Invocation

This spell simply throws a Ender Pearl

Arcane Swoop: Gon + Gon + Astral Invocation

Swoops the target off their feet launching them back and into the air

Arcane Snipe: Gon + Gon + Gon + Astral Invocation

This spell shoots a long range pulse of arcane energy at a target
